About

A bit about me

I graduated from the University of Tennessee (Knoxville) with a B.S. in Business Administration, majoring in Business Analytics with a concentration in Information Management Systems. I finished with a 3.71 GPA — but the number I'm prouder of is how fast I went from student to building real analytics infrastructure inside one of the country's most complex research institutions.

Before my current role, I interned at Johns Hopkins APL building Power BI dashboards for technical teams, and before that at NIH/NIAID where I managed federal grant workflows. That range — federal operations to advanced research analytics — gave me a practical fluency that I bring to every project.

I'm at my best when I'm translating between what the business needs and what the data can say. I like to build things that last, document everything, and make sure someone else can pick it up after me.
